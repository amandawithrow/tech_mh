Comfort with Supervisor

Employees who responded to the survey were relatively evenly distributed between levels of comfort discussing mental health issues with their supervisors. The level of comfort discussing these issues plays a role in whether or not people take advantage of resources, benefits or leave options at work.

Comfort with Coworkers

Interestingly, less people are comfortable discussing mental health with their coworkers than with their supervisors.

Negative Responses to Mental Health in the Workplace

By and large, employees did not report witnessing any negative responses to mental health issues in the workplace, suggesting no overt negativity.

Positive Responses to Mental Health in the Workplace

Based on the responses, by and large there have been mostly neutral to positive responses witnessed in the workplace.
