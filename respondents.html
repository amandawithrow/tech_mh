Respondent Ages

While the total number of respondents has changed from year to year, the trend in age has remained the same. The highest number of respondents fall in the 25-34 year old category, followed by 35-44. From a generational standpoint, we are seeing Gen-Xers as the most represented, followed by millenials. Few respondents were 55 or over.

Respondent Gender

While the total number of respondents has changed from year to year, the trend in gender remains the same. More men responded to the survey each year than wopmen. When you consider the average demographics of tech employees, this is not surprising, as men still outnumber women in the field. However, considering that women are more likely to be diagnosed with, or identify with, mental health issues, it is an interesting unexpected spin.
