Mental Health and Tech Companies

With so may digital companies trying to tackle healthcare in general, and behavioral health in specific, it warrants a closer look at the attitudes of both companies and employees around mental health. Open Sourcing Mental Health fields a survey yearly asking for employees of tech companies to weigh in. This project analyzes the data from 2016, 2017, and 2018 to look at trends and create actionable insight for employers.
