Company Sizes

While the total number of respondents has changed from year to year, we still have good (and mostly even) representation across different sizes of companies, with the exception being mid size (500-1000 employees) being underrepresented.

Company Mental Health Benefits

By a ratio of more than 2 to 1, companies are providing mental health benefits to their employees. It is interesting to note the size of "I don't know" responses, suggesting that if companies do in fact have benefits, they are not doing well at communicating about them. It should be noted that the year to year differences in responses are representative of changes in overall sample size.
